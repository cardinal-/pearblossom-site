Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Overview

Pearblossom is a photo composition app that guides you through capturing a series of photos and compositing them into a single grid-based image. This policy explains what data Pearblossom accesses and how it is used.

Data We Access

Camera

Pearblossom requires access to your device's camera to capture photos for each cell in your composition grid. The camera is only active when you are actively capturing a photo within the app.

Photo Library

Pearblossom requires access to your Photos library solely to save your completed composition to your device. The app does not read, browse, or access any existing photos in your library.

Anonymized Usage Data

Pearblossom may collect anonymized usage data to help us understand how the app is used and improve the experience. This may include:

  • Grid sizes selected
  • Number of compositions started and completed
  • General time of day and frequency of use
  • Device type and iOS version
  • App performance and crash data

This data is anonymized, not linked to your identity, and never includes photos or personally identifiable information.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your data to advertisers or third parties
  • We do not use your data for ad targeting
  • We do not share your data with marketing partners
  • We do not upload, transmit, or store your photos anywhere outside your device

Your Choices

You can opt out of anonymized usage data collection in the app's settings at any time.

Photos Captured In-App

Photos captured using Pearblossom's in-app camera are held temporarily in memory while you build your composition. Once you save your composition to Photos, the exported image is saved to your device's Photo library. When you start a new composition or close the app, all temporarily held photos are discarded from memory. Your photos never leave your device.

In-App Purchases

Pearblossom offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock additional grid sizes. Purchase records are handled entirely by Apple and are subject to Apple's privacy policy. Pearblossom does not store or process any payment information.

Children's Privacy

Pearblossom does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. Since no personally identifiable data is collected, the app is safe for use by all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated policy will be posted at this URL with a revised "last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
