Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Overview

Pearblossom is a photo composition app that guides you through capturing a series of photos and compositing them into a single grid-based image. This policy explains what data Pearblossom may access, how it may be used, and your choices regarding that data.

Data We Access

Camera

Pearblossom requires access to your device's camera to capture photos for each cell in your composition grid. The camera is only active when you are actively capturing a photo within the app.

Photo Library

Pearblossom requires access to your Photos library solely to save your completed composition to your device. The app does not read, browse, or access any existing photos in your library.

Usage Data

Pearblossom may collect anonymized usage data to help us understand how the app is used and improve the experience. This may include:

  • Grid sizes selected
  • Number of compositions started and completed
  • General time of day and frequency of use
  • Device type and iOS version
  • App performance and crash data

This data is anonymized and not linked to your identity.

Location Data

With your permission, Pearblossom may collect general location data (city or region level) to understand where our users are and to help us improve the app. We do not collect precise GPS coordinates.

How We May Use Your Data

We may use the data described above to:

  • Improve the app's features and user experience
  • Understand usage patterns and prioritize development
  • In future versions, serve relevant advertising or partner with third parties to provide anonymized behavioral insights

We will always be transparent about how your data is used and will update this policy if our practices change.

Your Choices

  • Analytics: You can opt out of anonymized usage data collection in the app's settings.
  • Location: Location data is only collected with your explicit permission. You can revoke this at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Pearblossom.
  • Advertising: If we introduce advertising or data partnerships in the future, we will provide clear opt-out controls and update this policy in advance.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. If we work with third-party analytics or advertising partners in the future, those partners will receive only anonymized, aggregated data — never photos, never personally identifiable information, and never precise location data.

Photos Captured In-App

Photos captured using Pearblossom's in-app camera are held temporarily in memory while you build your composition. Once you save your composition to Photos, the exported image is saved to your device's Photo library. When you start a new composition or close the app, all temporarily held photos are discarded from memory. Your photos are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared.

Children's Privacy

Pearblossom does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe your child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated policy will be posted at this URL with a revised "last updated" date. For significant changes, we will notify users within the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
